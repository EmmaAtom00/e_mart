## Recording Tips

### Before Recording:
1. **Clear browser cache** and start fresh
2. **Prepare test account** with some cart/wishlist items
3. **Test all features** to ensure they work smoothly
4. **Close unnecessary tabs** and applications
5. **Set browser zoom to 100%**
6. **Use a clean browser profile** (no extensions visible)

### During Recording:
1. **Speak clearly and at a moderate pace**
2. **Use smooth mouse movements** (no jerky clicks)
3. **Pause briefly** between scenes
4. **Highlight important UI elements** with cursor
5. **Keep video under 3 minutes** for engagement

### Tools:
- **Screen Recording**: OBS Studio, Loom, or built-in screen recorder
- **Video Editing**: DaVinci Resolve (free), iMovie, or Shotcut
- **Audio**: Use a good microphone or record voiceover separately

### Video Settings:
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 or 60 fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
